Our Promise to You

Every file you upload to EggyPDF is processed securely and permanently deleted from our servers immediately after your download is complete — and always within one hour. We never read, share, sell, or retain your documents. Your files are yours, and yours alone.

1 Introduction

At EggyPDF, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and tools located at eggypdf.com. We have written this policy in plain, straightforward language so that you can understand exactly how your data is handled.

By using EggyPDF, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

2 Who We Are

EggyPDF is a free online platform that provides PDF productivity tools and a Resume Builder to individuals around the world. EggyPDF is committed to building a trustworthy, privacy-first service that respects every user regardless of where they are located.

Throughout this policy, "EggyPDF", "we", "us", and "our" refer to the EggyPDF platform. "You" and "user" refer to any person who visits or uses our website.

3 Information We Collect

We collect the absolute minimum amount of information necessary to provide our services. We do not require you to create an account or submit any personal details to use any of our tools.

Files you upload: Any PDF, Word document, or image file you upload is stored temporarily on our processing servers solely for the duration of the task you have requested. These files are never opened, read, or accessed by any member of our team.

Anonymous usage data: We may collect non-personal, anonymous data about how our tools are used, such as which tools are most popular, the approximate country of visitors, browser type, and general device type. This information cannot be used to identify you personally.

Contact information: If you contact us directly via email, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and is not shared with any third party.

✅ We do not collect your name, address, phone number, payment details, or any other personally identifiable information during normal use of our platform.

4 How We Handle Your Files

The handling of your uploaded files is the most important aspect of our privacy commitment. Here is exactly what happens when you use any EggyPDF tool:

Upload: Your file is transmitted to our processing server via an encrypted HTTPS connection. This ensures that your file cannot be intercepted during transit.

Processing: Our server performs the task you requested, whether that is merging, compressing, converting, or any other operation. This is an automated process with no human involvement.

Download: The processed file is made available for you to download directly to your device.

Deletion: Both your original file and the processed output are permanently and automatically deleted from our servers. This deletion occurs immediately after your download is complete and in all cases within one hour of upload.

✅ No member of the EggyPDF team ever views, accesses, or reads the contents of your files. The entire process is fully automated.

5 How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes. To provide, operate, and improve our PDF tools and services. To understand how our platform is being used so we can prioritise improvements. To respond to any questions or support requests you send us directly. To maintain the security and reliability of our servers and infrastructure.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described above.

6 Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes. We will never monetise your data in any form.

Our platform relies on the following trusted third-party infrastructure providers to operate. Cloudflare Pages hosts our frontend website and provides content delivery and security services. Render.com hosts our backend processing server where file operations take place. Both providers are reputable, security-focused companies operating under strict data protection standards. Neither provider has access to the contents of your files beyond what is technically required to transmit data across their networks.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from authorities. In such cases, we will disclose only the minimum information required and will notify affected users where legally permitted to do so.

7 Security Measures

We take the security of your data seriously and have implemented the following measures to protect your files and information.

HTTPS encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS encryption. This prevents any third party from intercepting your files during upload or download.

Isolated storage: Uploaded files are stored in temporary, isolated directories on our server with no public access. Files cannot be accessed by other users or external parties.

Automatic deletion: Our system is configured to automatically delete all uploaded and processed files within one hour. This deletion is permanent and irreversible.

No logging of file contents: Our server logs do not record the names, contents, or any metadata of files beyond what is technically necessary to complete the processing task.

We may also use anonymous analytics cookies to understand how our tools are being used. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies that follow you across other websites. For full details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

If you believe that a child under 13 has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.
