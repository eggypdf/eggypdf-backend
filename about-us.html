About EggyPDF

We're on a mission to make PDF tools simple, fast, and free for everyone.

💡

Why We Built This

EggyPDF was born from a simple frustration. Most PDF tools online are cluttered with ads, locked behind expensive subscriptions, or just too complicated for everyday people to use. There had to be a better way.

🔒

Free Tools, Built Securely

EggyPDF offers a growing suite of free PDF tools including merge, split, compress, convert, watermark, protect and more. All tools run on a fast, lightweight backend so files are processed quickly and securely. Every uploaded file is automatically deleted from the server within one hour, because your privacy matters.

📋

The Resume Builder

The Resume Builder was built with one goal in mind: making it easy for anyone, whether a fresh graduate or a seasoned professional, to create a polished and job-ready resume in minutes. Smart suggestions, live preview, and instant PDF download. No design skills required.

🚀

Always Growing

EggyPDF is growing, and so is its toolset. More tools are hatching soon 🥚. From AI-powered summarization to digital signing, Excel conversion, and beyond. The mission is simple: keep every core tool free, always.
