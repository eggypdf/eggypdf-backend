Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the website owner. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information on their own.

EggyPDF uses a minimal number of cookies — only those that are strictly necessary for the website to function correctly and to help us understand how our tools are being used so we can improve them.

2 Cookies We Use

Essential

Session Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They allow you to navigate between pages and use our PDF tools without losing your progress. Without these cookies, services you have asked for cannot be provided. These cookies do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Analytics

Analytics Cookies

We may use anonymous analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us identify which tools are most popular, how long users spend on each page, and where we can improve the experience. All data collected is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

✅ We do not use advertising cookies. EggyPDF does not use cookies to track you across other websites, build advertising profiles, or serve targeted advertisements.

3 Why Cookies Matter for EggyPDF

As a free tool platform, understanding how our tools are used helps us make better decisions about which features to build next. For example, knowing that Compress PDF is used more than Watermark helps us prioritise improving compression quality for our users.

This information is collected in aggregate form only. We never track individual users or link cookie data to personal identities.

4 How to Control Cookies

You have full control over cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies at any time. You can also set your browser to block cookies entirely, though this may affect the functionality of some features on EggyPDF and other websites.

To manage cookies, look for the privacy or settings menu in your browser. Common browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge all provide cookie management options in their settings panels.

Please note that blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using certain tools on our platform. Blocking analytics cookies will have no impact on your ability to use any EggyPDF tool.

5 Third Party Cookies

EggyPDF is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and our backend runs on Render.com. These services may set their own technical cookies as part of their infrastructure. These cookies are outside our direct control and are governed by the respective privacy policies of Cloudflare and Render.

We do not embed third-party advertising networks, social media trackers, or marketing pixels on our website.

6 Changes to This Cookie Policy

As EggyPDF grows and adds new features, our use of cookies may change. We will update this Cookie Policy to reflect any such changes and will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

Questions about cookies?

If you have any questions about our use of cookies or how we handle your data, please get in touch with us.
